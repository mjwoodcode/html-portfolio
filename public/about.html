About Me

Some would say I'm an entrepreneur, but I just think I have too many interests to count. It can be difficult being a starving artist and unfortanately, music doesn't pay the bills. I get bored of things quickly; however, I have become newly interested in coding and I can confidently say that I have yet to get bored. There are so many new things to learn with code. I hope I am able to make it as a web developer. It would be a dream to pursue both music and development. This is my longterm goal
